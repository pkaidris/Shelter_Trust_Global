FAQ

  1. Who is the developer of shelter trust estates?
    1. Shelter trust global resources limited
  2. What is the price per plot for residential and commercial allocation?
    1. See our flyers for details.
  3. How will an increase in price affect an existing subscriber?
    1. An increase in price will not affect an existing subscriber in anyway expect if they subscribe for additional plots after price increase.
  4. What is the size of residential and commercial plots?
    1. See our flyers for details
  5. What happens if lesser or bigger size of land is allocated to me?
    1. In the case of lesser allotment in size, a sum in equivalent to the size in shortage multiplied by the size in square meters will be refunded and in case of a bigger size, the excess meters multiplied by the price per square meter will be demanded.
  6. What type of infrastructure will the developer provide?
    1. Shelter trust will provide perimeter fencing, earth road, street lights, centralized boreholes, drainage etc.

      1. Electricity, pole and strings.
      1. Documentation fee (2)
        1. Yet to be determined.

    3. When do I pay my development levy?
      1. Immediately after full payment is done for the land.
    4. What do I get as legal security for my property investment with ?
      1. Receipts of cash payments, contract of sales, deeds of assignment, letter and certificate of allocation, survey plan, global C-of- O.
    5. Is the road to the estate good and accessible?
      1. Yes, the road to the estate good and accessible at the peak of the rainy season.
    6. Is there any restriction as to type of building I can build?
      1. Yes. You are to build residential house as specified in your building plan within the area designed as residential and commercial houses within the area designed commercial. Provision of shops in residential houses and building of tenement house type (popularly known as face me i face you) is not permitted.
    7. What about the Omo ni-ile’s(Owners of the Land) intrusion?
      1. You will not encounter any intrusions from the Omo ni-iles(Owners of the Land) and nothing like incessant billings of Owo(Money) foundation, Owo(Money) window frame etc.
    8. Is there any limit to commence work on my land after allocation?
      1. Allotees are encouraged to take possession and develop their plots within two year of allotment in order to control weeds. Also, in the case of neglect, the developer will enter the land to keep it tidy and expenses incurred will be transferred to the property owner. Non development within this stipulated time frame could lead to revocation and re-allocation in other part of the estate.
    9. Is shelter trust global resources going to assist clients in accessing National Housing Fund(NHF) loan?
      1. Yes. It has a mortgage institutions that are ready to partner with us in providing NHF and other mortgage services.
