ABOUT US

Shelter trust Global Resources Limited, is an incorporated public limited liability company established under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, to carry-out the business of real estate management services. Shelter trust was born out of the need to provide housing deficit solutions in Nigeria and globally. The Company was established in 2017; with the ultimate goal of rendering excellent services, cost effective products and in line with international best practices (IBP).

We are genuinely client focused and are continually seeking improvements in our products in order to achieve our aims and objectives. We are determined to succeed and draw inspiration from challenges.

Our philosophy is provision of value added solutions that are both timely and cost-effective.Our staff are highly trained with many years of experience and are always available for all your queries and requests. You can be assured that Shelter trust is at the fore-front of providing your housing solutions.
