Vacancy

Shelter trust was borne-out of the need to provide housing deficit solutions in Nigeria and globally. We are genuinely client focused and continually seeking improvements in our products to achieve our products through our aims and objectives. Based on our recent expansion we are in need of the following positions:

  • Marketers
  • Accountant
  • I.T. Officer
  • Personal Assistant
  • Admin Officer
  • HR Personnel
Mode of Application

All applicants are to forward their CV with position applied for as subject matter to info@sheltertrustglobal.com on or before 20th Sept, 2018. Only candidates that live within Ikeja axis and its environs are advice to apply.
NOTE: All applicants except Sanitation Officer must be a computer literate.

DISCIPLINE: Applicant must have qualification in the following discipline;
QUALIFICATIONS: OND, HND & BSc.

  • Real Estate Management
  • Business Administration
  • Public Administration
  • Economics
  • Computer Science/Engineering

Marketers
  • Experience in real estate Marketing
  • Present and sell company products and services to current and potential clients to meet sales targets
  • Participating in marketing activities or events to raise brand awareness and create sales outlets
  • Ability to use the different social media platform for Marketing
  • Preparing weekly report based on progress made on Sales
  • Follow-up for collection of outstanding payments
PERSONAL ASSISTANT
  • Responding to a reporting manager’s email and responding if required
  • Preparing communications on behalf of a manager
  • Answering phone calls
  • Organising travel and itineraries
  • Organising and planning meetings
  • Taking notes and writing minutes during meetings
  • Conducting or preparing any research that the reporting manager may require
  • Various ad hoc requests
I.T. OFFICER
  • Should be able to design a website with WordPress
  • Should be a good digital marketer
  • Ability to update WordPress website
  • Installing and configuring computer hardware, software, systems, networks, printers and scanners
  • Monitoring and maintaining computer systems and networks
  • Responding in a timely manner to service issues and requests
  • Providing technical support across the company (this may be in person or over the phone)
  • Setting up accounts for new users
  • Repairing and replacing equipment as necessary
  • Testing new technology
  • Possibly training more junior staff members
ACCOUNTANT
  • Examining bank statements and reconciling them with general ledger entries
  • Examining expenses submitted by employees
  • Keeping an eye on incoming payments from accounts receivable and outgoing payments from accounts payable
  • Creating company financial reports with the above information included
  • Analyzing data collected in order to determine the state of the company’s financial Health
  • Analyzing data to understand where and when the company is generating and losing Revenue
  • Examining the proficiency of the software programs used to organize Data
  • Generating financial reports that display the company’s profits, equity and cash flow
ADMIN OFFICER
  • Prepare and transmit a variety of correspondence, real estate and lease related documents
  • Work with various corporate departments to coordinate maintenance, repair and due diligence of closed or leased properties
  • Serve as the first point of contact for client/prospect, provides timely responses to all inquiries and answers questions, as well as responsible for schedule showings and negotiation of closed stores and leased locations
  • Perform research to determine closed store values and/or lease values
  • Basic knowledge of real estate principles and processes
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook
HR PERSONNEL
  • Managing company staff, including coordinating and supporting the recruitment process
  • On boarding newcomers to the company
  • Determining suitable salaries and remuneration
  • Providing the necessary support systems for payroll requirements
  • Developing adequate induction and training
  • Supporting employee opportunities for professional development
  • Assisting with the performance management and review process
  • Must be a computer Literate
